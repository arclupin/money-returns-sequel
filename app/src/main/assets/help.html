Accounts

The accounts menu is where you can see all your accounts and the available balance for these accounts. By touching on an account, you can see all of the transactions that have occurred on that account, along with the amount of the transaction. You can get more information about a transaction by pressing the transaction you would like more info on. This will display the transactions ID, the account that was payed, the date and time of the transaction and the approximate location. Here you can also add the transaction to a group.

Payments

The payments screen is where you can pay another account. This can be someone else's or one of your own accounts.
